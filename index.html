Who I Am

Timothy's Story 👾


My professional journey began when I fell into front-end development while constructing an e-commerce website for a close family friend. What started as a manual task quickly became completely engrossing: I found genuine focus and enjoyment in mapping layout grids and coding responsive interfaces from absolute scratch.

"I started looking at games differently: instead of only asking where to find the blue collectible pendant in Resident Evil 4, I started asking how movement, systems, interfaces, and game feel actually work."

That core shift changed everything. I stopped simply consuming games and started study-building them. Today, under my visual brand marque TI-MAH, I combine interface rules, rapid game system prototyping, custom audio integration, and layout science. I am building my path between UI/UX Design, Game Development, Front-End engineering, and the TI-MAH brand to create digital experiences built with pure intention.
